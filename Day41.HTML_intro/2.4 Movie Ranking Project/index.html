Best Anime According to Dylan

Categories:

1. Shonen

-Naruto

It's a journey. A lot of drama, a lot of power and a lot of filler too. The whole package.

2. Mecha/Romance

-Darling in the Franxx

It was the first official anime I watched. The romance was justified by my lonely state.

3. Slice of Life

-Blue period

Quite artistically pleasing and very pleasant pacing. Genuine 10/10

4. Isekai

-Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation

Relatable main character, proper story telling.

5. Romcom

-Tomo-chan is a girl

It goes how you wan't it to go. Hence very very satisfying.
